## Description
The effects of colonisation on Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples

## Elaborations
* the doctrine of terra nullius providing legal justification for British colonisation of Australia without treaty or negotiation
* Frontier Wars, such as the 1834 Pinjarra Massacre and the 1894 Battle of Windjana Gorge
* the denial of Aboriginal rights enshrined through legislation, such as the Aborigines Act 1905 (Western Australia)
