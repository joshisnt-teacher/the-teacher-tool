## Description
Why and how participants in the global economy are dependent on each other; the effects of globalisation; and the impacts of global events on Australian consumers and businesses

## Elaborations
* global dependence through global supply chains and transnational corporations
* effects of globalisation such as availability of goods and services, access to larger markets, environmental degradation and local producers competing in the international market
* disruptions to global supply chains due to global events such as pandemics, natural disasters, international conflicts and economic crises
