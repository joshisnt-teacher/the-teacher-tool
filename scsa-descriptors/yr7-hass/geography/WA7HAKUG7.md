## Description
The strategies used to enhance the liveability of places for different groups in society

## Elaborations
* global shift in urban development that incorporates green space for residents
* providing accessible parking, ramps and signs in braille for people living with a disability
* improving access to the National Broadband Network for people living in rural areas to allow for use of services such as healthcare and banking
